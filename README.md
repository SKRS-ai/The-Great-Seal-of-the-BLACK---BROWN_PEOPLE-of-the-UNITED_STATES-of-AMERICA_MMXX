# The-Great-Seal-of-the-BLACK---BROWN_PEOPLE-of-the-UNITED_STATES-of-AMERICA_MMXX
The Thin Black &amp; Brown Lines (TBBL) USA nonprofit was established as a foundational pillar to house and protect the Great Seal of Black &amp; Brown People of the United States. This design concept, owned by Solomon Kin Records, serves as an official cultural mark—a "Seal of Black America"
