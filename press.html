FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE: PHILADELPHIA, PA

Solomon Kin Records Announces Launch of National Heritage Registry

PHILADELPHIA — In a historic move toward community sovereignty, Solomon Kin Records, in partnership with TBBLUSA, has officially unveiled the Great Seal of Black and Brown People of the United States and the accompanying Sovereign-Village-OS.

The initiative, described as a "Solution-Based Bureau," aims to provide a documented identity framework for justice-involved individuals, creating a path toward federal contract readiness and community restoration.

"We are not just building a logo; we are building a national infrastructure," says the Bureau. "From Philadelphia to Minnesota, the Pillars of America represent a new era of documented goodness and economic power."

For more information, contact the Bureau of National Identity at TGSOBAUSA.ORG.
